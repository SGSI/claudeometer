What you get

A fuel gauge for your Claude limits.

No more guessing how much you have left. Claudeometer reads your live quota and turns it into something you can read in half a second.

Live quota, gradient color

The 5-hour window right in the menu bar, with a color that shifts smoothly from green to red as you climb — no hard jumps.

Burn rate & ETA

See how fast you're spending (+%/hr) and a forecast of when you'll hit the wall — turns a static gauge into a warning system.

24-hour pace graph

A sparkline of your recent consumption, built from history kept locally for 30 days. Spot your heavy hours at a glance.

Graduated alerts

Notifications at 50%, 75%, 90% (panic), and 100% of the 5-hour window — each fires once, so you're warned, not spammed.

Every quota at once

5-hour, 7-day all, Sonnet, Opus, OAuth apps, and extra-usage spend — all in one tidy popover.

Hot Claude Code sessions

See which of your local Claude Code sessions are burning the most tokens in the last five hours.

Share Claude · Team mode

Out of quota? Share your team's Claude.

Turn on team mode to pool Claude usage across your teams — create or join as many as you like, see everyone's live 5-hour usage, and request a window from anyone in your team who's less tapped out than you right now. No fixed cutoff, just whoever has more room. Opt-in and self-hosted.

Live boards for every team

Pick a team from your list — or an All view across them — and see each teammate's current usage and when their 5-hour window renews, so you always know who's using less than you before you ask.

Create or join teams

Spin up a team with a name and password, or join one you know the name and password for. Be part of several at once — say KC-Tech and KC-Product — and borrow within any of them. Public teams are discoverable and let people ask to join with owner approval; private teams stay invisible.

Borrow, end-to-end encrypted

Request a fixed window from anyone in your team who's less used than you — they get a notification with Approve and Reject built in. The credential is sealed just to you — the relay only ever carries opaque ciphertext — and it auto-reverts when the time's up. You can only borrow from someone you share a team with, and a borrow you make in one team stays private from your others.

Self-hosted & private

Runs against a tiny relay you host yourself. Teams are gated by a password (hashed on the relay), and the relay only ever sees usage numbers and opaque ciphertext — never your Claude credentials. It stays dormant until you point the app at your own relay.

How it works

Local, private, no setup.

Claudeometer piggybacks on the login you already have. Your token and usage data never leave your Mac — there's no account and no telemetry.

Reads your existing login

It uses the OAuth token Claude Code already stores in your macOS Keychain. Nothing new to sign into.

Checks your quota

It calls Anthropic's usage endpoint for your live numbers and keeps a small 30-day history locally for the pace graph.

Keeps you in the loop

The menu bar updates itself, and you get a nudge at each threshold — so a hit limit is never a surprise.

Install macOS 13+

Homebrew — recommended; opens with no Gatekeeper warning:

brew tap sgsi/claudeometer https://github.com/SGSI/claudeometer.git brew trust sgsi/claudeometer brew install --cask claudeometer

Installed before July 2026? The tap moved into this repo — if you see "remote mismatch" or upgrades say you're up to date when you're not, re-point it once:

brew untap --force sgsi/claudeometer brew tap sgsi/claudeometer https://github.com/SGSI/claudeometer.git brew trust sgsi/claudeometer brew reinstall --cask claudeometer

Or download the .dmg — drag Claudeometer to Applications. It isn't notarized yet, so clear the quarantine flag once:

xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/Claudeometer.app
